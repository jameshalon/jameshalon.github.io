Live Streams

I am currently running two series of live sets that air at different times.

The first is Melodic Dreams, a monthly series streamed live the first weekend of the month on Twitch.tv and Kick.com. I record the session and the replay is available on YouTube, SoundCloud and Mixcloud the following Monday morning.

The second series is called Breakdown Therapy. Unlike previous series, this is an unrecorded live experience focusing on being a laid back hang where I talk with Twitch chat and play tunes. This series is streamed every Tuesday @ 4:30pm Pacific Time on Twitch.tv and Kick.com. Make sure to follow to get notifications when each series starts!

  • Melodic Dreams First weekend of the month
    Progressive & Melodic House. Lush soundscapes, relaxing vibes, and deep bass-lines.
  • Breakdown Therapy Every Tuesday @ 430-600pm Pacific Time
    Wandering through all things House x Techno, a laid back hang session talking about anything that comes up in the chat and playing tunes.

P.S. If you like what you hear, please consider becoming a patron. I record every Breakdown Therapy session and as a patron you will get access to these exclusive sessions each week. Check it out!

Biography

contact & booking: contact @ jameshalon.com

promos: promos @ jameshalon.com

DJ and producer of House, Melodic House, Progressive House & Techno.

Based in Riverside CA, past residences in San Diego CA, San Francisco CA and Dallas TX.

Starting out under the moniker DJ3 circa 1996, James Halon was a resident at Record Resource and played events in Denton and Dallas TX. Moving to San Francisco in 1999, James started weekly events with For The Music & helped found the music side of False Profit.

James ran the sound system for False Profit at 43 Norfolk and put on many amazing nights at the warehouse. He has also joined forces and played with many crews and venues, including Dub Beautiful, Ambient Airlines, Space Cowboys, B-Family, Eklektic, Future Breaks, Tenderloft, Tags Trance Trip, GrooveTech Radio, Anon Salon, Trust, Wish, Mighty SF, DNA Lounge, 1015 Folsom, and many more.

2004, James co-founded Cedub Studios and moved away from DJing to focus on production and engineering for local artists. During this time he helped create and launch Fake Science as a podcast, then expanded to online digital music distribution and A&R. He produced tracks under the Cell Culture act with Chris Walcott. He also produced tracks as The Clockmakers with DJ Yap, under the moniker DJ Bespoke.

In 2009, James moved from the Bay and headed to San Diego. After taking a longer than expected break to settle into his new home, James built out his studio and has returned to producing and DJing. Starting in 2020, James began weekly live streams, featuring new, classic and original tracks focusing on all things House x Techno. In the summer of 2021, James was invited to become a resident of the San Diego based collective Digital Cocoon.

In the summer of 2022, James once again packed up and this time moved to Riverside, CA. There he has been able to expand his music studio and streaming rig, allowing him more time to write, produce and play music. He still regularly travels to San Diego to support and participate in Digital Cocoon events and other area shows.
