Live Streams

I am live streaming DJ sets two times a week on Twitch.tv and Mixcloud. I have programmed each day to have a unique style, similar to how a club will book out. It's a chance for me to play different music I love and keeps it fresh for all of us. Every session also has paired VJ mixes using HalonVision, which is a custom built programmatically generative mixing system that creates a new and responsive experience every time.

If you miss a session, you can replay them at my YouTube channel or on Mixcloud.

  • Melodic Dreams Tuesday @ 5pm - 6pm Pacific
    Progressive & Melodic House. Lush soundscapes, relaxing vibes, and deep bass-lines.
  • Umbra Thursday @ 5pm - 6pm Pacific
    Driving, Underground & Dark Techno. Locked in at a 130bpm.
